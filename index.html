Welcome to the SBCC Computer Science Club!

We are a group of people ranging from casual computer enthusiasts to passionate hardcore programmers. Our club encourages our members to get together, share and learn programming skills and general computer usage.

We also go on field trips, tour the large local tech companies and connect you with potential employers in the field of the tech industry.
